PREPROCESSING STEPS
  1. HTML stripped using BeautifulSoup
  2. Email headers (From/To/Subject/Date) removed
  3. Whitespace normalized
  4. Duplicates removed
  5. Texts under 20 characters removed
  6. Classes 0 and 1 capped at 5,000 each; class 2 kept as-is (currently small)
  7. Stratified 70/15/15 train/val/test split (random_state=42)
